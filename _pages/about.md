I am a Ph.D. candidate in Operations Research at Southern Methodist University (SMU) under the supervision of **Prof. Halit Üster**. **I am on the 2026–2027 academic job market looking for Assistant Professor positions.** My graduation is expected in May 2027.

My research concentrates on developing large-scale optimization frameworks and decomposition algorithms to solve critical problems in transportation, logistics, and disaster management. My doctoral work has been supported by the **National Science Foundation (NSF)** and recognized with the **Moody Dissertation Fellowship**. 

Beyond research, I serve as the President of the **SMU INFORMS Student Chapter**, where we foster community and professional growth in operations research and data analytics.

### 🛠️ Core Research Interests
* **Methodologies:** Large-Scale Optimization, Decomposition Algorithms (Benders Decomposition), Mixed-Integer Linear Programming (MILP), Time-Expanded Networks.
* **Application Areas:** Transportation & Logistics, Emergency Response & Disaster Management Logistics, Retail Management.
